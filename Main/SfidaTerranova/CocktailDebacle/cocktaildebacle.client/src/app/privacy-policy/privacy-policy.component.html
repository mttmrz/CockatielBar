Privacy Policy & Cookie Policy

Cookie Policy

Last update: 18/03/2025

1. Introduction

This website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance the user experience and personalize content based on browsing preferences. This Cookie Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how you can manage your preferences.

2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They can be used to ensure the website functions, analyze traffic, or personalize the user experience.

3. Types of cookies used

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the operation of the website.
  • Customization cookies: Store searches made and items added to favorites to improve future suggestions.

4. Data collected

We collect anonymous information about searches made and items added to favorites. This data is stored exclusively in our database and is not shared with third parties.

5. Managing preferences

You can deactivate personalization at any time through your account settings or via the cookie banner on the website.

6. Contact

For more information, you can contact us at: cockatiels@info.it

Privacy Policy

Last update: 18/03/2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect user information in accordance with the GDPR and other data protection regulations.

2. Data collected

  • Anonymous data: Searches made on the website, added to favorites, and interactions with content.
  • Non-anonymous data: Only the site administrator has access to data associated with users to improve the experience.

3. Purpose of data processing

The collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Customize content shown to users.
  • Improve the browsing experience.
  • Analyze general behavior on the site.

4. Legal basis

Data processing is based on the user's explicit consent, which can be revoked at any time.

5. Data retention

Data is stored in our database and is not shared with third parties. Users can request the deletion of their data at any time.

6. User rights

Users can:

  • Request access to their data.
  • Request modification or deletion of their data.
  • Withdraw consent for content personalization.

7. Contact

To exercise your rights, contact us at: cockatiels@info.it
